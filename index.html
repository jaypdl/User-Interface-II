My Projects

I started learning web development because I wanted something different for my life. I was working 6 to 7 days out of every week, 60 to 80 hours on average, sometimes 100. I worked in the oil-field an Industrial Radiographer, basically: I x-rayed the pipes to make sure they were welded together properly and wouldn't leak or explode. It was good money, but it meant barely any time with my family. It meant getting up at 4am and sometimes getting home at 11pm, sometimes we had to stay overnight. I was always on-call with no guaranteed days off. So, the Pandemic happenedand the oil field slowed down, and at first I was furloughed, and then eventually laid-off. But, this has given me the chance to re-invent myself. So, here I am.
